Magazines

Finemusic

Finemusic is a monthly magazine published by the Friends of Radio 4 Society and officially endorsed by RTHK Radio 4. It mainly covers the programmes to be aired on Radio 4 and related topics. The printed version is available free of charge at major performing arts venues.

hkclassical.com [in Traditional Chinese only]

A classical CD and DVD review site maintained by Lam Wai Kau. Reviews are brief but a new entry is added daily.

* * * * *

Foreign magazines such as Gramophone, Fanfare, International Record Review, etc., are available at HMV, Hong Kong Records, Shun Cheong and some bookstores.

In addition, many local Chinese audiophile magazines (e.g. Hi-Fi Review, Audiotechnique) have brief music/review sections.
