Introduction

The purpose of this guide is to give overseas classical music lovers a concise overview of Hong Kong's classical music scene. Hopefully after reading this guide, they will know where to go for concerts and CD-shopping if they happen to visit Hong Kong.

I do not have any affiliation with the organizations and record stores mentioned below, nor do they endorse this page in any way. I have no intention to make this page a research paper, but I did put a lot of effort to verify my information and make it comprehensive. However, if you find any mistake on this page, as well as additional information/suggestion, please do not hesitate to contact me at . I will also update this page if I find new information to add or errors to correct.

For more information about Hong Kong in general, you should visit the following web sites as well:

  • Hong Kong Government Information Centre
