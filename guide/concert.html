Concerts

Besides the Hong Kong Philharmonic Orchestra, famous soloists and orchestras also perform in Hong Kong regularly. Tickets are usually reasonably priced because the government is either subsidizing or organizing most of these concerts. Its Leisure and Cultural Services Department ("LCSD") maintains a list of upcoming cultural programmes at the following address:

  • Cultural Programmes Search

The LCSD's Cultural Presentation Section also maintains a list of selected events with more details.

Usually pamphlets advertising these concerts with ticket information are available one month in advance at the major cultural venues and URBTIX outlets. The LCSD maintains a list of locations, business hours and phone numbers of these ticket outlets at the following address:

  • URBTIX

Tickets to some of the cultural events can also be ordered online. Another ticket-booking network is HK Ticketing.
