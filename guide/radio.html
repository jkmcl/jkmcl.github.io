Radio Stations

Radio Television Hong Kong operates Radio 4 (FM 97.6-98.9MHz), the only classical music station in the region. Besides classical music, its programmes also feature traditional Chinese, jazz and world music, as well as drama and arts news. Its presentation format is primarily bilingual (English and Cantonese).
